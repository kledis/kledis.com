FRAC PAC

An increasingly popular preparattion offered by coffee service
providers is known as a frac pac. This is ground coffee in
a self-contained packet, enveloped by a filter. This allows a
conventional drip coffee maker to be used, but without the mess
of cleaning out the old grounds and without the requirement to
measure out the right amount of coffee. The user only needs remove
the old pack and to place the new pack into their coffee machine.
The downside of such a service is quality of the coffee. Unless canned,
roasters will generally not have a filter enveloping machine, these
packs are of roasting and within four hours of grinding for the best
quality. Since local shipped great distances and may be weeks old
before they even arrive at the office.
