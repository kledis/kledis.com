similar to latte and cappuccino
(and more a mix of the two), a large cup
with (today) espresso and steamed milk
with some milk foam. Melange and

A CREAMY BULLHORN
SERVED WITH DARK
CHOCOLATE

Bullhorns have been traced to the Arabs during
the Emirate of Sicily, with a possible origin for the word
and recipe deriving directly from qanawāt. These were deep
fried dough tubes filled with various sweets, which were a
popular pastry across the Islamic world at the time.
